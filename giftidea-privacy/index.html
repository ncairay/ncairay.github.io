GiftIdea - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

GiftIdea does not collect any personally identifiable information. All gift searches are processed anonymously. We do not require account registration.

2. How We Use Information

Anonymous usage data may be collected via Firebase Analytics to improve app performance. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

3. Third-Party Services

GiftIdea uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google) - anonymous usage statistics
  • OpenRouter API - AI-powered gift recommendations
  • Affiliate links (Amazon, Klook) - purchasing recommendations

Some links in the app are affiliate links. These do not affect our gift recommendations.

4. Data Storage

Search history and saved gifts are stored locally on your device only. We do not store any user data on our servers.

5. Children's Privacy

GiftIdea is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
